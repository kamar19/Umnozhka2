MultiplyIt

Multiplication table and other mathematical operations. Simulator

The Multiply application offers to solve mathematical problems in a playful way to consolidate educational material for children of different ages, as well as their parents.
The game is free!
In Russian, English and Kazakh languages!

The minimalist style was not chosen by chance, there is nothing superfluous. Few sounds and a minimum of pictures. Rather, only one in the form of a heart shows the number of lives earned for correct answers.
You can choose what mathematical actions will be in the tasks: multiplication, division, addition, subtraction.
You can choose numbers with which you have difficulty in multiplying and dividing.
Solving simple tasks, the child will easily learn them!
In the "Results" section you can see your training results.
Download for kids and learn math easily.
Adults will also find it useful to work with 7s and 8s, and mental addition of two-digit numbers will definitely appeal to them.
